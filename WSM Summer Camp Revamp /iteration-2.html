— 08

Answers to everything else.

    Does my child need any prior experience?
    Absolutely not — and most don't. About 68% of our campers have never played an instrument before. Beginners and advanced students are placed into small groups with musicians at a similar level. No one gets lost, no one gets held back.
    Which instruments can my child learn?
    Voice, guitar, keyboard, bass, and drums. Kids try things on Monday and land on a primary by Tuesday — it's totally fine to show up not knowing which one.
    Does my child need to bring their own instrument?
    We encourage it (practicing on the same instrument you rehearse on matters), but we have loaner instruments on a first-come basis for families who don't have one yet.
    How does registration and payment work?
    Pick your sessions above, check out online with a 50% deposit. The remaining balance is charged to the card on file on the first day of each session. No phone tag.
    What's the cancellation policy?
    Cancel 14+ days before your session starts for a full deposit refund. 7–13 days: 50% refund. 0–6 days: deposit is non-refundable. Family illness is handled case-by-case.
    Do you offer aftercare?
    Yes — 3:30–5:00 PM, $25/day, no commitment. Add it day-of or for the whole week. Private lessons after camp are also available as a separate program.
    Will there be lunch breaks?
    Yes. Lunch + Community Hour runs 12:15–1:15 daily. Kids bring their own or order delivery as a group. This is also when friendships form — which, no exaggeration, is half the reason kids come back.
    Where can we park?
    Parking lot along the western wall of the school. Additional parking along 29th Street and 13th Ave.
